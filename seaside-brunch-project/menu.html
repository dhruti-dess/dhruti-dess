MENU

STARTERS

bruschetta

Our wood-fired bruschetta is made with TLC, chosen for our menu by our friend Miley. When she was in Rome she had the opportunity to attend a cooking class and learn to make authentic Bruschetta. She said it was the best food she has ever tasted in her life and brought back the recipe for us. Our bruschetta is served with fresh grape tomatoes, balsamic reduction, and EVOO.

farm fresh salad

Our farm fresh salad was chosen by our friend Lily. She grew up on a farm and has sworn by this salad her entire life. As she has grown and tried new things, she has tweaked and perfected this recipe which we now bring to you. Our salad is made with butter lettuce, vine tomatoes, purple cabbage, yellow bell pepper, sprouts, sweet potato, guava, avocado, chickpeas, and our secret tahini dressing.

egg & avocado toast

Our egg & avocado toast is sweet and simple, chosen by our friend Emily. She found this toast in a random café in Maine one day and has loved it ever since. This is the start to meal every time she visits us. Our egg & avocado toast uses cage-free organic grade A eggs and are boiled to perfection and laid on top of multigrain toast and a bed of fresh ripened avocados.

nachos

Our nachos were chosen by our friend Jackson, he loves his game food. He found this recipe visiting a sports bar many years ago and drunkenly fell in love with these nachos. He took us back there so we could get a taste of it for ourselves and see what he saw in this beautiful platter. We made an even better iteration of it, our nachos are made with ground beef, hatch queso, pickled onions, and pico de gallo, and our house guacamole, sour cream, and some more delicious pico de gallo is served on the side.

MAINS

burrata & arugula wood fired pizza

Our wood-fired arugula & burrata pizza was chosen by our friend Mikayla who had the chance to find this masterpiece in Italy. This pizza is loved by everyone and devoured in seconds by guests. Mikayla says that whenever she grabs a bite, it takes her back to days abroad. Our arugula & burrata pizza is made with arugula & burrata on top of san marzano tomatoes and drizzled with honey.

salmon

Our salmon was chosen by our friend Robby. He said that this will be the staple and that our seaside restaurant needs to have something amazing from the sea. He tried this dish for the first time in Japan and we have had the pleasure of being introduced to the chef who created the recipe. Our salmon is oven-baked and pan-seared, and then garnished with fresh citruses and cilantro.

gnocchi

Our gnocchi is a family recipe chosen by our friend Oliver. He chose this recipe because he believes everyone should get to taste something as amazing as his Nona’s gnocchi. It truly is a wonderfully delicious dish. Our gnocchi are made to order, sauteed in a butter and sage sauce, and topped with pan-roasted walnuts to give it some crunch.

wagyu burger

Our wagyu burger was chosen by our friend Jesse. When he traveled to Japan he had the privilege of trying one of the best burgers in the entire world. Fortunately, he told us about it and now we are also in the running. We import our meat from the same Japanese port and have the pleasure of bringing it to you. Our burger is made with Japanese wagyu, tomatoes, slaw, sharp cheddar, and some toasted brioche buns.

DESSERTS

cheescake with chocolate curls & caramel drizzle

Our cheesecake was chosen by Hannah, one of our owners. She said she dreamt of this cheesecake every day since the first time she had it in France. She has good taste because this cheesecake is undeniably spectacular. Our cheesecake is a secret recipe that we concocted ourselves in an effort to recreate Hannah’s dream, and it is drowned in chocolate curls and drizzled in a rich caramel sauce.

rasberry & cream cake

Our raspberry and cream cake was chosen by Sarah, she is a pastry chef and created this recipe herself. She says it is for those of us that like desserts that are not too over the top sweet. Our pastry chef at the restaurant followed Sarah’s lead and says it is her favorite menu item. Our raspberry and cream cheesecake is made with fresh picked raspberry from a local farm, and hand whipped cream, it is piped in between three layers of a crepe-like cake and tastes amazing.

lava cake

Our lava cake was chosen by our other owner, Jean. This ooey gooey chocolate lava cake was stumbled upon by her when she was in London, she went back to the same place three times in that same week because she could not get enough. We made a beautiful creation that Jean says tops the original. Our lava cake is made with a Ghirardelli chocolate filling and sprinkled with powdered sugar.

blueberry & banana french toast

Our blueberry & banana french toast was another drunk find. It was chosen by our friend Joel who decided to order this along with his bottomless mimosas at brunch one day. He says it is the most incredible thing he has ever had, and he would eat it at any time of the day. Our blueberry & banana french toast is made with fresh handpicked blueberries and bananas and made with our house made white bread.
